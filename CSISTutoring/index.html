Tutoring Guidelines

Drop-in CSIS tutoring is available to all Siena CSIS students in Roger Bacon 350 (open lab).  Tutors are typically available Sunday-Thursday night from 7-10pm.  The schedule below outlines the regular tutor on duty each night, possible subs, and the courses for which they can provide tutoring services.
